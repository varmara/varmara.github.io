About me

I am a Senior lecturer at Department of invertebrate zoology ( Faculty of Biology, St Petersburg State University ).

I’m interested in various aspects of marine ecology: structure and dynamics of marine communities; role of interspecific interactions in communities; assessment of anthropogenic impact on coastal and marine habitats; microevolutionary processes in sympatric populations of closely related species; methods of statistical analysis.

Interests
  • Community ecology
  • Evolutionary ecology
  • Data analysis
